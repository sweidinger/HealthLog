## 6. German medical terms — NATURAL (one Low consistency nit)

The new chart/metric labels read as natural German, not literal machine translation:

  pulse_wave_velocity → "Pulswellengeschwindigkeit", vascular_age → "Gefäßalter",
  lean_body_mass → "Magermasse", fat_free_mass → "Fettfreie Masse" — all correct
  clinical German.

### LOW — DE double-support term diverges within the app
`messages/de.json`: coach metric label `analytics.metricLabels.walking_double_support`
= **"Doppelstützzeit"**, while the chart page (`charts.walkingDoubleSupport.*` +
`navWalkingDoubleSupport`) = **"Doppelstandphase"**. Both name the same HealthKit metric
(`HKQuantityTypeIdentifierWalkingDoubleSupportPercentage`). Apple Health's own German
localization uses "Doppelunterstützungszeit". Not wrong, but the same metric gets two
different German names in two surfaces — a user comparing the Coach prompt list to the
chart nav sees a mismatch.
Fix (optional, post-release acceptable): pick one term across both surfaces. Recommend
"Doppelstützzeit" (shorter, matches the coach label) for both, or align to Apple's
"Doppelunterstützungszeit". Other locales are internally consistent for this metric.
